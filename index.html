About This Screen

Create a voucher, fund it with Lightning, then share the claim link. Unclaimed sats automatically return to your Lightning Address once the voucher expires.

Voucher Expiry

The expiry timer starts when the voucher is first funded, not when it is created. Each time you add more funds, the expiry resets from that point.

Personal note

It is never sent to the server — it's stay on your device only. The note appears in your history so you can track individual vouchers.

How it works

  1. Create a voucher Choose the voucher settings.
  2. Fund the voucher Pay the Lightning invoice from your Bitcoin wallet.
  3. Send the link Share the claim link so they can redeem the sats.
  4. They claim the sats If they don’t claim in time, the sats return to your Lightning Address.
