How to claim your sats

What are sats?

Satoshis ("sats") are the smallest unit of Bitcoin — like cents to a rand. There are 100 million sats in one Bitcoin. You can save them, spend them, or send them to anyone in the world instantly.

How do I claim them?

  1. Get a Bitcoin wallet — download Blink (recommended for beginners) from the App Store or Play Store.
  2. Tap "Show Voucher" on this page to show the QR code.
  3. Scan or tap the QR code in your wallet app to receive the sats.

What can I do with them?

Spend at thousands of merchants, pay bills, send to friends, or hold as savings. See the wallet guide below for more details.
